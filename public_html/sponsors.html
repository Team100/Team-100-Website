Sponsors

The Sequoia Union High School District houses Carlmont and Woodside High Schools among others and is a generous sponsor of Team 100.

Pentair Thermal Management is a thermal engineering company dedicated to innovation and customer satisfaction.

TE Connectivity Foundation designs and fabricates products for a variety of fields including but not limited to electronics and mining.

The Carlmont PTSA (Parent Teacher Student Association)'s works hard to bond the home and school so parents and teachers can work together to educate their children.

The Tate Family has been a very generous, faithful sponsor of Team 100.

Peninsula Infiniti is a car dealership located in Redwood City that sells new, certified pre-owned Infiniti, and used vehicles.

Intuitive Surgical is a company that works in the field of robotic-assisted minimally invasive surgery. Their corporate headquarters are located in Sunnyvale, California, and they serve customers internationally, providing technology and procedures across many different fields.

Harmonic Drive LLC is a company that leads the high precision gearing and motion control industry with their servo actuators, gearheads and gear component sets. They provide high precision, high performance solutions to customers' application requirements.

SRI International is a non-profit, independent research and innovation center serving government and industry. Their research and innovations have led to new industries and products that impact people's lives daily.

NTT Group's mission is to find communication services that satisfy everyone and contribute to a fully productive society.

NASA is an organization that works in four principal organizations, called mission directorates: aeronautics, human exploration and operations science, and space technology.

Alan Steel & Supply Company is a company that sells inexpensive, quality scrap metal.

SolidWorks is a company that offers 3D software tools which let you create, simulate, publish, and manage your data.

Various relatives, close friends and family also provide many helpful donations to our team!
