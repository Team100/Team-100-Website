Sponsors

The Woodside High School Foundation is a generous sponsor of Team 100.

The Sequoia Union High School District houses Woodside High School among others and is a generous sponsor of Team 100.

Pentair Thermal Management is a thermal engineering company dedicated to innovation and customer satisfaction.

Intuitive Surgical is a company that works in the field of robotic-assisted minimally invasive surgery. Their corporate headquarters are located in Sunnyvale, California, and they serve customers internationally, providing technology and procedures across many different fields.

The Tate Family has been a very generous, faithful sponsor of Team 100.

The Searcy Family

Solutions 2 Projects is a consulting firm created to support a growing need for controlled IT systems in the biotech industry.

Woodcraft delivers quality woodworking tools, supplies and services.

Barnes & Associates Insurance Agency works hard to provide insurance and believes that the best way to help you obtain the right coverage - at the best possible price - is to help you understand what you need to know about insurance.

Microsemi offers a comprehensive portfolio of semiconductor and system solutions for communications, defense & security, aerospace and industrial markets.

SRI International is a non-profit, independent research and innovation center serving government and industry. Their research and innovations have led to new industries and products that impact people's lives daily.

Recology San Mateo County manages municipal disposal processes and services that span the needs of urban, suburban and rural communities.

John R. Shroyer and Jane L. Shroyer

Alan Steel & Supply Company is a company that sells inexpensive, quality scrap metal.

SolidWorks is a company that offers 3D software tools which let you create, simulate, publish, and manage your data.

Chucks Donuts sells Donuts, Pastries and Coffee

Various relatives, close friends and family also provide many helpful donations to our team!
